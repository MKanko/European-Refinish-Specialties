About Us

Over 35 years of vintage and contemporary European car body and paint refinishing experience. From light cosmetic small damage touch up repairs to full body frame off and paint restorations. Professional stock or custom interior and exterior painting, wheel surface repair, personalized black out services and customized body modifications, Paint Protection Film and Detailing.

Specialties

Specializing in German and Italian Automotive Refinishing, Paint Protection Film application, Motorsport Vehicle Graphics, BASF Glasurit Single Stage & Multicoat Clearcoat OE Finishes, Small Paint Damage Repair and Professional Automotive Detailing.

Founder and owner Nicholas Mario Ruiz developed a passion and artist eye for design and engineering on Porsche, BMW, Mercedes Benz and Ferrari at a very young age, “I sought out repairing and restoration almost exclusively on German and Italian cars in the early 1980’s.” Mario and the crew enjoy working and sharing with clients who appreciate the finer details of the craft.

BASF Glasurit Small Paint Damage Repair

This system is a patented chemical system developed by BASF Glasurit in Germany years ago. It is a system used for factory new car and in port transportation damage repair touch ups. It has worked so well over the years BASF launched it for repair shops specially to address the small dings and scrapes to use less time and materials saving money and adding value for our customer.

PDR "Paintless Dent Repairs"

PDR is a tool to address some specific smaller dings& dents. It can save time and money when compared to ordinary body shop repairs. The process can also be used together with BASF Small-Damage Repair as a way to finesse and contain a repair saving both time, material and add value for specific repairs.
